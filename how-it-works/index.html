Watch: How to post a /match (30 sec)

1

Type /match and tap send

Open any channel in Slack and type /match. This opens the match form. Works on phone or desktop.

2

Fill in the details

When: Today, Tomorrow, or pick a future date

Where: Choose a court from the dropdown

Looking to: Match, Rally, or both

Flexible: Open to switching time or location?

Notes: Optional — "court reserved" or "9am–12pm"

3

Tap "Post It"

Your match card goes live in your borough channel. Future dates also post to #future-play.

4

Another player taps "I'm in!"

You'll get a message from the PARTNER MATCH App the moment someone accepts. DM each other to confirm details and get on court.

Pro tip

You'll also get suggested matches automatically when another player wants to play on the same day — even if you didn't see each other's posts.
